Our Mission

Our web application serves as a centralized platform for reporting and resolving maintenance issues across our campuses. Students and faculty can easily report issues, track progress, and receive updates, ensuring a safe and functional learning environment for all campus members.

We are committed to providing an efficient and user-friendly platform for reporting maintenance issues on campus. Our goal is to ensure a safe and functional learning environment by streamlining the reporting and resolution process.

ARE YOU A STUDENT?

Register & Log In

Register using their dut4life email and a chosen password upon initial app use.

Log In to your account to access the app features.

Report Issues

Once logged in, submit your issue via the "Report Issue" tab, specifying campus, block, and exact location.

Provide a brief summary and select an appropriate issue type from the dropdown.

Once you click submit, you're good to go! Our maintenance team will promptly address the reported issue.

Upvote

You can explore the list of current via our "View Issues" tab.

You have the option to upvote issues they encounter, prioritizing them for resolution.

Ensure that you are logged in to utilize our upvoting system.

ARE YOU A TECHNICIAN?

Log In

Log In with your technician credentials to access to the technician dashboard where you can view assigned issues, update their status, and communicate with users.

View Assigned Issues

Access the list of issues assigned to you and view detailed information about each issue, including its status, priority, and location.

Update Issue Status

Update the status of assigned issues as you work on them. You can mark issues as in progress, resolved, or escalate them if necessary.

Communicate with Users

Communicate with users who have reported issues to gather additional information, provide updates on the progress of their reported issues, and resolve any concerns they may have.

ARE YOU AN ADMIN?

Log In

Log In as an admin to get access to the admin dashboard where you can manage users, view and resolve reported issues, and perform administrative tasks.

Manage Users

Add, edit, or remove users from the system.

View and Resolve Issues

Access the list of reported issues and take necessary actions to resolve them. Assign issues to technicians, track their progress, and communicate with users regarding their reported issues.
